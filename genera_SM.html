SYNAPTIC MANOEUVRE

~ Dance to the rhythm of your dreams ~

Description

Charge up your mind to solve the riddles to open the gateway to Bio realm! After entering ,surf your memories to find the pathways through the realm and use your craftmanship to create and explain the pathways to the fellow realm members.

Round - 1 (Bio-riddle)

  • ● Riddle based on technical topics will be given.

  • ● The technical topics will revolve completely based on basic biology.

  • ● 20 riddle questions will be provided.

    • Round - 2 (Cyclic perturb)

  • ● Cyclic perturb is a memory based round.

  • ● 2-3 biological pathways/cycles( flowcharts) will be given with blanks in it

  • ● Participants have to fill in the blanks within the time limit and clues will be there provided

  • ● Points will be deduced based on each clue. max(5)

    • Round - 3 (Cytosculptor)

  • ● Cytosculptor is an creativity and critical thinking round

  • ● participants will be provided with clay.

  • ● A biotechnology based theme will be given.participants have to model the clay based on the topic.

  • ● Participants have to model the clay based on the topic and must give an explanation lecture for abt 1-2mins.

    • Rules & Regulations

    • ● For round 1 : The team that gets more points in the riddle round will get more priority and star questions do carry additional points.

    • ● For round 2 : Clues can be given,provided points will be deduced for each clue taken. (max-5 )

    • ● For round 3 : Students with the shortest time and effective content will be declared winners

    • ● The Google Form link will be opened only at that particular time and will not accept responses beyond the stipulated time.

    • ● Participants who qualify for the final round will be intimated by the respective coordinator.

    • ● Participants who bag the first three places will receive elite certificates and cash prize upto RS.3000.
